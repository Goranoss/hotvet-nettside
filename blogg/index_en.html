Blog

Health information and guides from Hotvet legesenter

Welcome to our blog. Here you will find practical guides, answers to common questions, and up-to-date health information for patients — or anyone considering becoming a patient — at Hotvet legesenter in Drammen.

Our goal is to make it easier to navigate the Norwegian general practitioner system: changing your GP, understanding your rights through Helfo and Helsenorge, preparing for your appointment, and knowing when to get in touch. We also cover preventive health, vaccinations, travel vaccines, and how we handle urgent care during opening hours.

The blog is updated regularly with new posts from our doctors and team. Is there a topic you would like us to write about? Please reach out via our contact page.
