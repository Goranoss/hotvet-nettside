Free card: When you have paid NOK 3,115 in co-payments in a calendar year, you receive a free card automatically from Helfo — and pay no co-payments for the rest of the year. Children under 18 and pregnant women at pregnancy check-ups are exempt.

How much does it cost to see a GP?

Co-payments are the same for all GPs in Norway and are set by Helfo and Parliament. Prices update when new rates take effect on 1 July each year.

Payment and no-show fee

You can pay by card, Vipps or invoice. A no-show fee applies if the appointment is not cancelled at least 24 hours before. Questions about a bill? Get in touch — we'll gladly explain.
