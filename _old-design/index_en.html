Frequently asked questions

Can I switch my GP to Hotvet Medical Centre?+

Yes! Anyone can switch GP via Helsenorge.no using BankID. It is free and takes just a few minutes. Registration takes effect on the 1st of the following month.

When can I book an appointment after switching?+

You can book an appointment from the date you are registered with us — that is, from the 1st of the month after you switched. Until then, you remain a patient with your current GP.

How much does a consultation cost?+

A regular daytime consultation costs 179 NOK in patient co-payment. Specialists in general medicine have an additional 56 NOK. Once you reach the free-card limit (NOK 3,115), you pay no co-payments for the rest of the year.

What do I do if I need urgent care outside opening hours?+

Call the emergency room (legevakten) at 116 117. For life-threatening situations, call 113.

Can I take blood tests at the centre?+

Yes, sampling is done directly at Hotvet Medical Centre. Analyses are sent to the hospital laboratory in Drammen for testing.

Do you offer e-consultation?+

Yes! You can start an e-consultation via Helsenorge.no once you are registered as our patient. You log in with BankID.
