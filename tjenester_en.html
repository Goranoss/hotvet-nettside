SERVICES

Our services

We offer a broad range of medical services in general practice, including urgent care for conditions that cannot wait. As part of Norway's GP scheme, we provide safe and accessible care for all patients on our list.

GP consultation

Thorough examination, diagnosis and treatment plan. Standard appointments booked via Helsenorge.

Urgent care within opening hours

Conditions that cannot wait are prioritised the same day. Call us at 31 02 13 00.

Blood samples and laboratory tests

Taken directly at the centre. Analyses sent to Drammen hospital laboratory.

Vaccination

Travel vaccines, flu, HPV and the childhood immunisation programme.

Sick notes and certificates

Sickness certificates and medical statements when needed.

Mental health

Consultations, follow-up and referral to psychologist or DPS when needed.

Specialist referral

Referral to specialist care when clinically indicated.

E-consultation

Written, video or phone — booked via Helsenorge with BankID.

Minor surgical procedures

Removal of moles, sutures, biopsies and other minor procedures.

Women's health, pregnancy and contraception

Pregnancy check-ups, smear tests, contraception and gynaecological examinations.

Digital services via Helsenorge.no

Many of our services are available digitally. Log in with BankID — or call us at 31 02 13 00.
